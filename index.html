About Us

This is GenSI Lab, the Generative Symbolic Intelligence Group affiliated with Tsinghua AIR, with some members also associated with the Shanghai AI Lab. Our long-term goal is to achieve AGI by creating AI scientists.

The pages shows a research series of Bayesian Flow Network(BFN) done by GenSI, focusing on both theoretical innovations as well as practical applications.
